As a social organisation, we have been contributing to the Kwahu community from the inception of our Lets Tour Kwahu Our Leader of Experience, Bright, started as a volunteer in Obomeng Palace, where he commenced with setting up social initiatives, like the Elderly Foundation , distribution of litter bins ,HIV/HPT health screening , Sanitation awareness, Girl Child Education & Mentorship and a Community Library Project. We have been working with partners and our doors are always opened to individuals and organisations who have any plan to impact the Kwahu communities in the areas of Education ,Sports, Entrepreneurship and Tourism.
